About Us

Welcome to our website! I am Arnav Thete, the creator of this platform, with invaluable support from Pranav Ghaywat, who has provided all the exam papers featured here.

This website is dedicated to offering previous year preliminary exam papers, as well as the boards 2024 question papers and NCERT 2025 sample papers, all in one place to help my batchmates prepare for their Boards 2025 exams.

Our aim is to make exam resources easily accessible to aid students in their practice. This initiative operates under fair use, aligning with school regulations and serving solely to support academic preparation.
