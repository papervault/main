Why Use This Website?

The official CBSE site does not offer access to last year's preliminary exam papers, and the process of downloading previous year question papers (PYQPs) is cumbersome. Our site allows for direct PDF downloads, simplifying the process. We also provide exclusive access to preliminary exam papers that are not available anywhere else on the internet, making our platform a unique and valuable resource.
